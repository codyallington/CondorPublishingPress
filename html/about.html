About The Author Maria Gamero Allington

I find Native American Culture fascinating. Most of the native nations showed respect to mother nature. The members of these communities learned their traditions orally and many of their messages involved a Great Spirit that could see all of their actions.

The Natives designed the messages from the Great Spirit in geo-glyphs like the Nazca Lines. Scientists are still looking for the meaning of these giant figures on the south coastal side of Peru. The purpose of my books is to share the purpose of those Lines.

My sources are original. They have not gone through any changes. They are currently almost as preserved as when they were first discovered. My sources have been considered spectacular sites of the planet. Among them and most importantly are the Nazca Lines. The people who created these advanced glyphs suggest that they were witnesses of a great force and their intention was to share them with people from the future. The attention to detail in their work, materials, and common symbols of this mystery and others are what interest people from all parts of the world today.
